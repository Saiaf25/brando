About Elite

Elite is an Egyptian farming, manufacturing, and export house specialised in frozen vegetables and fruits. We grow and source from our own Nile Delta and Upper Egypt farms, process in our own Elite Fruit Manufacturing facility, and ship through an unbroken cold chain — so importers get consistent quality, honest grading and competitive pricing from one accountable partner.

We currently ship to partners across Africa, Europe, Russia and Asia, and we treat the first container with a new client as the start of a long relationship, not a one-off trade.
